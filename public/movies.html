The best movies according to Rafael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Revenant

1822. Hugh Glass (Leonardo DiCaprio) leaves for the American West willing to earn money hunting. Attacked by a bear, he is seriously injured and is left to fend for himself by his partner John Fitzgerald (Tom Hardy), who steals his belongings. However, despite all the adversity, Glass manages to survive and begins an arduous journey in search of revenge.

Dad's Home

Brad (Will Ferrell) is a radio executive who strives to be the best stepfather possible to his girlfriend Sarah's (Linda Cardellini) two children. But then Dusty (Mark Wahlberg), the children's foul-mouthed father, reappears and begins to compete with him for the kids' attention and love.

La Sociedad de la Nieve

Inspired by a true story and based on the book of the same name by Pablo Vierci, The Snow Society talks about the case that occurred in 1972, when Flight 571 of the Uruguayan Air Force, chartered to take a rugby team to Chile, crashes into a glacier in the heart of the Andes. Only 29 of its 45 passengers survive the accident. Trapped in one of the most inaccessible and hostile environments on the planet, they are forced to resort to extreme measures to stay alive.
